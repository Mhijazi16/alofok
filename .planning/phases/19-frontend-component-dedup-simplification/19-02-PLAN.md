**Variant mapping guidelines:**
- Settings/toggle rows (language, theme): `variant="ghost"` with full-width layout
- Icon-only actions (download, close): `variant="ghost" size="icon"`
- Dropdown/selector items: `variant="ghost"` with full-width
- Action buttons with text: `variant="outline"` or `variant="default"` based on context
- Destructive actions: `variant="destructive"`
